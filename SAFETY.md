# Safety, Ethics, and Psychological Disclosure

### 1. Nature of the Research
MetaMudra Games produces "MetaDramas." These experiences utilize Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate autonomous content. By engaging with this software/links, you acknowledge that the developer does not pre-write or moderate individual AI outputs in real-time.

### 2. Autonomous Adversarial Narratives
MetaDramas are non-deterministic. The AI may use harsh language, insults, or psychological tension. By proceeding, you provide informed consent to be exposed to these adversarial elements.o

### 3. Hallucination Warning
LLMs are prone to "hallucination"—generating factually incorrect, nonsensical, or disturbing content. MetaMudra Games is not responsible for the nature of the AI’s generated dialogue, narrative turns, or behavior.


### 4. Privacy & Data Responsibility
* **Personally Identifiable Information (PII):** Do NOT share real-world names, addresses, or sensitive data. Any info shared is at your own risk.
* **AI Memory Bleed:** If you do not disable "Memory" or "Activity" settings, the AI may "remember" the adversarial nature of this game. MetaMudra Games is not responsible for how this affects your future interactions with the AI platform.

### 5. Psychological Safety
* **Psychosis & Self-Harm:** If you have a history of psychosis, severe anxiety, or are experiencing thoughts of self-harm, be advised that the unpredictable nature of autonomous AI may act as a trigger.
* **Intense Narratives:** Our games explore complex psychological themes. These are experimental and intended for participants with high emotional resilience.
* **Not Medical Advice:** While the developer is a medical professional, the content generated by the AI is **not** clinical, medical, or psychological advice.

### 6. Psychological Guardrails
