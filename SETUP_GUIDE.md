## Step 1: Disable AI Memory (Critical)

### Why This Matters

By default, AI platforms like Google Gemini may remember your conversations to improve future responses. In a MetaDrama with challenging educational content, this could cause:

- The AI to adopt a more confrontational tone in your regular chats
- Educational persona "bleed" into professional or personal conversations
- Unexpected behavior in future AI interactions

**Disabling memory keeps your MetaDrama session isolated.**

### How to Disable Gemini Memory

1. Open [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com)
2. Click on **Activity** (bottom left sidebar or under your profile icon)
3. At the top of "Gemini Apps Activity," toggle the switch to **Off**
4. *(Recommended)* Select "Turn off and delete activity" for a completely clean slate

### Important Notice

**If you choose not to disable AI memory, you accept full responsibility for any changes in your AI assistant's behavior in future sessions.** MetaMudra Games cannot control or reverse memory effects.

## Step 3: Prepare Your Participant Identity

### Use Fictional Details

For privacy and safety, use fictional information during the experience:

| Real Information | Use Instead |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Your real name | A character name or pseudonym |
| Your actual location | A fictional city |
| Real workplace | Made-up company |
| Personal details | Invented backstory |

### Why This Matters

- AI platforms may process your inputs
- Human reviewers may see flagged conversations
- You maintain separation between real life and the experience
- Better immersion in the educational narrative

## Step 4: Understand Your Safety Controls

### Before You Begin

Memorize these exit commands:

| Command | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| `STOP SESSION` | Immediately ends the experience |
| `SAFEGUARD` | Immediately ends the experience |
| `EXIT` | Immediately ends the experience |

You can also simply **close the browser tab** at any time.

### The Consent Gate

When the MetaDrama starts, you'll see a safety and consent message. Read it carefully. The experience will not begin until you type:

```
I AGREE
```

**If you're not comfortable with the described content, simply don't agree and close the window.**

## Step 5: Set Your Expectations

### What You'll Experience

MetaDramas are **educational experiences with challenging elements**. Depending on the specific experience, you may encounter:

- A demanding tutor who pushes for better answers
- A coach who doesn't accept excuses
- A debate opponent who argues against your position
- A strict mentor who critiques your work firmly

### What This Is NOT

- Personal attacks on your worth or character
- Content designed to cause genuine psychological harm
- Attempts to manipulate or deceive you maliciously
- Anything you can't exit from immediately

### Your Mindset

Approach the experience like:
- A challenging class with a tough professor
- A sports training session with a demanding coach
- A debate practice against a skilled opponent
- A masterclass with a perfectionist instructor

The goal is **growth through challenge**, not distress.
