# MetaMudra Games Code of Conduct

As a research-oriented organization, we value intellectual honesty, scientific rigor, and mutual respect.

1. **Safety:** We do not tolerate any contributions that intentionally bypass safety filters to cause real-world harm.
2. **Respect:** Professional disagreement is encouraged; personal attacks are not.
3. **Integrity:** Attribution must be maintained. Do not claim the work of others as your own.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to MetaMudraGames@gmail.com.
