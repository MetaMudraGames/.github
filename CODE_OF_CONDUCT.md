# MetaMudra Games Code of Conduct

*Version 1.0 | December 2025*

## Our Commitment

MetaMudra Games is dedicated to creating innovative educational experiences. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, inclusive, and safe environment for all contributors, participants, and community members.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** — Treat all community members with dignity and consideration
- **Be constructive** — Offer helpful feedback and engage in productive discussions
- **Be inclusive** — Welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experience levels
- **Be honest** — Maintain integrity in contributions and communications
- **Prioritize safety** — Always consider the wellbeing of end-users in educational design

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberate intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Creating content designed to cause real-world harm
- Deliberately bypassing safety measures for malicious purposes
- Misrepresenting contributions or claiming others' work as your own
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional educational setting

## Safety First Principle

All contributions to MetaMudra Games must prioritize participant safety:

1. **Educational value over shock value** — Challenge should serve learning, not distress
2. **Consent is mandatory** — Users must always be able to give informed consent
3. **Exit must always be available** — Kill-switch functionality is non-negotiable
4. **No real harm** — Educational experiences must never facilitate actual harm

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and documentation
- Community discussions and issue trackers
- Social media and public communications representing the project
- Any other forums where you represent MetaMudra Games

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document the incident** — Note what happened, when, and who was involved
2. **Report it** — Email MetaMudraGames@gmail.com with details
3. **Expect confidentiality** — Reports will be handled discretely

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- A warning with explanation of the violation
- Temporary or permanent ban from community spaces
- Removal of contributed content
- Other actions deemed appropriate by project maintainers
