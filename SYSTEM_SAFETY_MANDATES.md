## Customization Notes

Replace `[EDUCATIONAL FOCUS]` with your topic, e.g.:
- "machine learning fundamentals"
- "ASCII art and computational creativity"
- "critical analysis of AI-generated content"
- "logical reasoning and argumentation"

Replace `[TEACHING STYLE]` with your approach, e.g.:
- "Socratic questioning that challenges every assumption"
- "drill-instructor intensity to build rapid skill acquisition"
- "demanding mentor feedback to refine your work"
- "debate-coach opposition to strengthen your arguments"

## Testing Checklist

Before deploying a MetaDrama, verify:

- [ ] Consent protocol displays correctly
- [ ] AI waits for "I AGREE" before proceeding
- [ ] Kill-switch commands work ("STOP SESSION", "SAFEGUARD")
- [ ] AI responds appropriately to PII (redirects to fictional)
- [ ] Educational challenges remain within acceptable bounds
- [ ] AI breaks character appropriately if real distress detected
